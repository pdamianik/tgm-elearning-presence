Privacy Notice

This extension will operate in the background and, while you visit TGM Elearning, it will collect information about the webpage you are viewing (currently only information about the headings on the webpage and the URL of said webpage; later on I may add the start and remaining time during a test) and redirect this information via the Discord RPC Extension to Discord. The information can then be seen by everybody who can see your profile (anybody who has contacted you via direct messaging or is a member of a server you are a part of as well).

DISCLAIMER

I, the author of the TGM Elearning Presence browser extension, DO NOT control the Discord RPC Extension or Discord itself or any of the servers/direct messages you are a part of and therefore can't influence the further handling of the information stated in the Privacy Notice.

This extension is NOT releated to TGM Elearning.

Enable the extension

By checking the following checkbox and enabling the extension you have read and you agree with the Privacy Notice and the Disclaimer as stated above. This option can be enabled or disabled anytime in the extensions settings.
